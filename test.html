Rating: 2/5

Pros: Cheap, responsive

Cons: I suppose I should have looked closer at the pictures before I purchased, but this keyboard has a weird layout.

There is no space between the arrow keys and the insert/home block, with the Print Screen, Scroll Lock, and Pause/Break keys right above those, also without any space. If you use those keys on a normal keyboard, be ready for frustration.

Also, the \ key is moved up next to the numbers for some inexplicable reason, making backspace into a letter sized and out of the way key and the enter key into an unnecessarily large, odd shape.

Finally, above the block of keys with the arrows/lock keys, and home/paging keys is a set of three narrow keys which seem like extra F-Keys, but aren't. Instead, they're power keys which put the computer to sleep, wake it up, or shut it down. I have repeatedly shut my computer down when I meant to press F12 because I reach for the F-keys by feel. It drives me insane.

Overall I would not recommend this keyboard due to its odd layout

Cons: Backspace key is tiny.

Other Thoughts: I had to get one of these when I found out a client's computer didn't use PS/2 at all. Newegg shipping was of course awesome, so it didn't slow me down in the least.
